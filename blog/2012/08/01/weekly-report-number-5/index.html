Weekly Report #5

Aug 1st, 2012

Not much to say in my weekly report this week. I continued my work on the model, went to Geneva for a family meeting (so couldn’t work those days) and more of my work was merged into master.

I also dove back into FolksIndividual’s code to add the display_name property. Since the last time I worked on it, Jeremy Whiting had made some progress on my first patch. I’ve now submitted an updated version (bug #651672).

I couldn’t make it to GUADEC but I saw the (pdf version of the) GNOME Outreach Program Yearbook 2012 and it’s great! Thanks to Daniel Siegel and all the people who made this happen!
